## Overview

The Aggregate is the write-model of the Core domain. It receives commands from the client side and emit and append events into the [[container|EventStore]].


> **Note:** [[event|TransactionImportCompleted]] and [[event|TransactionImportFailed]] are **not** emitted by this aggregate. They are written directly to the event store by the [[service|EventInjector]] after the [[service|EnableBankingAPIGateway]] delivers the Enable Banking API response.
